Buffalo Chicken

What is it?

What is Buffalo chicken and how is different than any kind of chicken? The main disticntion between buffalo chicken and it's counter parts is the sauce. Buffalo sauce was invented in 1964 by Teressa Bellissimo when she decided t cook a late night snack for her sons and their friends. As it turns out they love it so muched it was on their families restaurants menu the next morning.

How does it taste?

Buffalo is a pepper based sauced with a few added ingriedients Your typical hot sauce is made of cayenne pepper, vinegar, and salt. When making Buffalo sauce you then add butter,and worcesterire sauce among other ingridients to make waht is now known as buffalo sauce. This meal is best paired with a cool side suh as celery to cut the eat, or even an additional sauce such as ranch also pairs well.

Buffalo sauce ingridients

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons distilld white vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestire sauce
  • pinch of granulated garlic

Recipe

  1. Add butter, hot sauce, vinega, Worcestire sauce, and garlic to a bowl
  2. Whisk together thorughly
  3. Store in an airtight container within the fridge
