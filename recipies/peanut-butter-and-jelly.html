Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

A classic

A timless classic the peanut butter and jelly sandwhich. This sandwhich had been around forever and is still one of the best meals largely due to its versitility. You can have it for breakfast,lunch, or diner and it will still hit the spot all the same.

Ingrediants

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly/Jam
  • Bread

Recipe

  1. Place two peices of bread on the counter.
  2. Lather one side with peanut butter.
  3. Then do the same thing to the other with jelly.
  4. Stick them both together.
  5. enjoy!
